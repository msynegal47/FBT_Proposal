The System

Your job is to
record. Everything
else is handled.

The workflow is designed to be as light as possible on their end. The less friction in the recording process, the more consistent the content.

1

Monthly Planning Check-In

Align on upcoming themes, calendar moments, and which avatars to lean into. Talking points and prompts are built out before any recording happens so they're never sitting down wondering what to say.

2

Record and Send - Weekly

Short clips recorded on your phone and sent over weekly via shared Drive or Dropbox. Talking points are ready in advance so you always know what to say. No overthinking, no big production days required.

3

Edit, Caption, Build

Raw clips become polished short-form reels. The same content feeds carousel posts that go deeper on the same idea - one recording, multiple pieces of content.

4

Schedule & Manage via Meta Business Suite

Everything queued, scheduled, and posted across Instagram and Facebook. Always working 2-4 weeks ahead so there's never a gap in the calendar.

5

Monthly Reporting & Quarterly Lookback

Monthly performance snapshot covering reach, engagement, and top-performing content by pillar. Quarterly deep-dive to see what's resonating and where to double down.
